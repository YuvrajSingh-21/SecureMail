Privacy Policy

Transparency and control over your data. Last updated: July 2026.

1. Introduction

At SecureMail, we take your privacy as seriously as we take your security. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform. Our core philosophy is to process the minimum amount of data necessary to protect your inbox.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register for an account, we collect your email address, name, and securely hashed authentication credentials. We use this strictly for providing access to the platform.

Google Authentication (OAuth)

SecureMail integrates directly with the Gmail API via standard OAuth2 protocols. We do not store your Google password. We only store the temporary access tokens provided by Google, which can be revoked by you at any time.

3. Email Processing & AI-Assisted Analysis

To provide our core threat detection features, our intelligent engines temporarily process your emails:

  • Local Processing: Emails are fetched directly to our secure servers, analyzed in memory, and delivered to your dashboard.
  • AI Threat Detection: We analyze linguistic patterns, sender headers, and structure. The AI generates explainable insights without logging the body of your emails permanently.
  • SafeLink: URLs are temporarily extracted and scanned against threat intelligence feeds.

We absolutely do not sell, rent, or share your email contents with third parties. We do not use your personal email data to train public AI models.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies strictly necessary for authenticating your session and keeping you logged in. We do not use third-party advertising trackers. For more details, please see our Cookie Policy.

5. Data Security & Retention

Your data is protected by industry-standard encryption both in transit (TLS) and at rest. We retain your account information only for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we immediately purge your tokens and profile data from our active databases.

6. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. You may export or delete your account at any time from your settings dashboard.

7. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@securemail.com.
