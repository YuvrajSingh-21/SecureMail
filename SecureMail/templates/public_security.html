Security by Design

How we protect your inbox, your data, and your privacy.

Our Security Philosophy

We believe security shouldn't be a trade-off with usability. SecureMail is engineered to provide enterprise-grade protection silently in the background, only surfacing when you need to make critical decisions.

Authentication

We do not store your email passwords. All email access is brokered through secure OAuth2 tokens directly from your provider, which you can revoke at any time.

Secure Email Processing

Incoming emails are analyzed strictly in memory. We neutralize tracking pixels and sandbox all links before rendering them to you in an isolated environment.

Privacy First & Responsible AI

Our AI threat models are built responsibly. Your personal emails are never used to train global AI models, and our classification algorithms are designed to be explainable and transparent.
